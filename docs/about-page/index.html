Our Mission

Challenging perceptions and empowering non-verbal individuals through innovative technology and research.

Breaking Barriers in Communication

Today, non-verbal children are often misunderstood and their cognitive abilities underestimated. Project Voice is here to change that narrative. We're proving that communication knows no bounds - even without words or signs.

Our Approach

We integrate cutting-edge computer software with biomedical engineering and neurology to explore non-verbal and nonsymbolic communication through brain monitoring applications.

Technology

Building upon the foundations of BCI2000, an open-source project, we're developing innovative solutions that bridge the gap between thought and expression.
