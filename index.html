What is CINPCT?

The Colloquium for the Improvement of Nonverbal Patient Care Technologies (CINPCT) is a groundbreaking and unique scientific meeting between researchers, clinicians, and students to develop and advance technologies for nonverbal patients. The purpose of this colloquium is to bring together distinguished researchers engaged in fields of study which are pertinent or could be applied in the field of Nonverbal Patient Care technologies, in order to:

  • Establish connections between attendees of this conference to lead to the application of their respective research to this field;
  • Foster dialogue among scientists across disciplines and across methods to raise awareness of the gap in research surrounding Nonverbal Patient Care technologies;
  • Engage Graduate and Undergraduate students involved in this research area in networking with professors and faculty at other institutions to pursue interest within this field.

Our mission is to engage more academics in the field of Nonverbal Patient Technology improvements by identifying research which could have applications in the field and encouraging new research collaborations to bridge the gap in scientific understanding surrounding this topic. CINPCT takes an interdisciplinary, multifaceted approach to broadening the discussion on Nonverbal Patient technology and encouraging dialogue between distinguished researchers on how to include more aspects of this problem in their future research.
