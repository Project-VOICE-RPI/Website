At-a-Glance

The 2025 CINPCT conference will take place on the 14th November 2025 at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.

This colloquium is a unique discussion between industry, academia and students to bring together multidisciplinary engineering and science for the improvement of nonverbal patient technologies. This one-day event is dedicated to looking at the underrepresented in healthcare, and the many ways that currently evolving science and technology can improve the lives of people today.
